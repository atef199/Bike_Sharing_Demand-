During EDA we gained some insight about how features are distributed. We can clearly see that season, holiday, workingday, weather columns have discrete distribution which signifies the fact that they represnt categorical features so it may be better to tell AugoGluon that these are categorical features. Another obsrvation I made was that perhaps we can make our model work better if we extract year,montu & day from datetime feature.
